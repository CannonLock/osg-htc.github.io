How to get an OSPool Account

In order to access the compute and storage capacity that is provided by the Open Science Pool (OSPool) researchers are asked to apply for an OSPool account.

To create an account three things must apply to you and your research:

Have a Scientific Application in Mind

All applicants should provide an idea of what they will be using the OSPool resources for.

Be, or be collaborating with, a U.S. based research project

The OSPool may only be used for work relevant to research and/or education efforts associated with an academic, government, or non-profit institution in the United States.

Meet with a Research Computing Facilitator

On completing your application a Research Computing Facilitator will reach out to discuss your computing needs and guide your next steps.

Please watch for an email from support@opensciencegrid.org to coordinate this meeting.

Apply for an OSPool Account

You can apply for an OSPool account today on OSGConnect.
